ABOUT

#GalaxyGameJam

Grab your smartphone and explore distant galaxies! At the #GalaxyGameJam you can create your very own Android games using only our Pocket Code app and become part of our mission to outer space. The best astronauts will receive awesome prizes!

Our mission to outer space

Together with Samsung we make a trip into space. But we need your help! The #GalaxyGameJam invites you and your friends to create a game, animation, or any kind of other appusing Pocket Code and experience the mysteries of the galaxies on your smartphone.

With Pocket Code we aim at inspiring kids, teenagers, and young adults to create their very own games and interactive stories directly on their smartphones. The intuitive "Lego"-style interface allows everyone to program without any previous knowledge.

Pocket Code is free on Samsung phones worldwide

EVENT

Details

This game jam is carried out in cooperation with Samsung. Your game created with Pocket Code can be submitted until the end of the European Code Week on 23.10.2016.

Theme

It’s all about our mission to outer space! Create your own games and stories about distant galaxies. Don’t forget to take the colourful characters Lynx, Panda, and Pingu with you on your journey.

Social Media

Follow us on our social media channels and stay up-to-date.

Who can be part of our mission to outer space?

You can either take part as a single creator or in a team of up to 4 creators. Depending on your age you will be assigned to a suitable category ( <12 years, 13-17 years, 18+). If you work in a team, the age of the oldest team member will be taken into account. You can also participate together with your classmates in the category “School Class”. In this case your class will be able to submit multiple programs.

How can I be part of the mission?

More detailed information about the registration process can be found in the section “Participate”.

What can I win if I participate?

Thanks to the support of Samsung we are able to reward selected astronauts with great prizes. Create your very own program that contributes to our mission to outer space and upload it with the Pocket Code app! Take your chance to win a new computer, tablet, smartphone, robot or other awesome prizes.

PARTICIPATE

How to participate?

Decide whether you want to work on your own or in a team. If you want to participate together with your classmates, please inform your teacher. Follow the “First Steps and Rules” and start creating your Pocket Code project.

How to learn coding?

If you don’t have any experience in coding yet don’t be afraid, it’s easier than you might think! The “Help” button inside the Pocket Code app guides you to a tutorial overview where we provided some great examples for beginners. Furthermore there is a big community behind Pocket Code that provides a huge variety of programs on the web. You can find them by clicking the “Explore” button inside the Pocket Code app.

Find the right materials for your program

To make it easier for you we provide special media assets which you may use during the game jam. Please read through our FAQs to get more information about the media library.

First steps and rules

  • If you participate as a single creator you can skip this part. If you work in a team, please choose a team name. If you want to participate as a whole class, please contact your teacher so he can fill in the application form.

  • First, let’s have a look at the media library in which we provided some awesome graphics for you. You can use them to make your program more colourful and they will hopefully give you some good ideas for your game. Of course you may use third-party graphics, but please respect copyrights and license requirements.

  • As the program has to include the outer space and distant galaxies theme you will have to think about what kind of program (game, animation, etc.) you want to create. You might already have some great ideas after having a look at the media assets.




  • In order to make your program even more interesting you can implement the following functionalities:


    ★ A welcome screen
    ★ A short description
    ★ A game over screen
    ★ A game won screen
    ★ Use at least one sensor
    ★ Create at least two levels
    ★ Use collision control
    ★ Use a foreign language

    • Submission

    After you have finished your game, please add a description that tells us what your game is actually about. Include the hashtag #GalaxyGameJam in your description. If you work in a team you need to include the team name as hashtag too (#Your Team Name, without spaces in the hastag).

    Please sign up on pocketcode.org if your are not registered yet.

    Now upload your game to pocketcode.org via the Pocket Code app. You can improve your app as often as you want and upload it again under the same name later.

    After your game has been uploaded you can open the game’s page. During this event we add a button for the submission. Press this button to submit your project.

    Please also answer the questionnaire for the submission. After that your program should be displayed on the Wall of Fame.
